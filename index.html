Community Needs Assessment

Fellow Deltans As part of efforts to address the challenges of development in our dear State, we have developed this web app/survey to get your opinion/inputs on what government should do to address the personal and community needs of Deltans because of our firm belief that development should be approached from bottom-up and not top-bottom and we promise that when we form government by the grace of God and wish of our people, we would implement the feedback from you.

You need to participate in governance and become part of the decision making process only if you identify your community needs and bring them to government for implementation and actualization. Community needs could be social infrastructure such as housing, health care, roads, schools, portable water, recreational facilities and many more.

Your identity is not known and your response will be treated as anonymous.
Thank you for your anticipated participation.
