Wetin Una Want?

A Community Needs Assessment Portal

Developed by DEMA
for

His Excellency, Senator Ovie Omo-Agege

Fellow Deltans, as part of efforts to address the challenges of development in our dear State, we have developed this web app/survey to get your opinion/inputs on what government should do to address the personal and community needs of Deltans because of our firm belief that development should be approached from bottom-up and not top-bottom, with a strong commitment from His Excellency, Senator Ovie Omo-Agege to put the feedback into use when he forms government by the grace of God and wish of our people in line with his mission to “Put Delta First” anchored on his “EDGE” Programme to “Build A New Delta”.

By filling this questionnaire, you will not only participate in governance, but also become part of the decision making process by identifying your personal and community needs, which could be social infrastructure such as housing, health care, roads, schools, portable water, recreational facilities and many more and bringing them to government for implementation and actualization.

Your identity will not be known and your response will be treated as anonymous.

Thank you for your anticipated participation.
